Title

Kenny and Joseph are best friends since elementary school. They often hang out together. One day, they decided to go on a vacation. They were both excited and look forward to it. But before they were able to go on the vacation, they were brought into an abandon house in the woods near their town. The doors in the house are all locked. Panic and fear came to them, they decided to find another way to leave the house. But, will they safely escape the house with the dangerous creatures that live in the house? Join with Kenny and Joseph on their adventure in the haunted house.
